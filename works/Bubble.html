Blow Out

Global Game Jam 2025 - Bubble

Blow Out

25/01/2025 - 26/01/2025

Unity

Game made in 48h for the Global Game Jam with the theme Bubble. Use your microphone to create bubbles. Best use of Input prize in Birmingham University, Birmingham, UK.

Team

  • Ki Chi Tong Tyron (Arist)
  • Raghav Prasanna (Artist)
  • Marc Morlas (Programmer)
  • Carlos Cordero (Programmer)
  • Guillem Llorach (Programmer)

Mechanics

  • Blowing into your microphone to create bubbles

Download on Itchio


More info

The game consists of blowing into the microphone to generate bubbles that catch falling objects and send them upward. Occasionally, a shark appears to try to take your lives.
