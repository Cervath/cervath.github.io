Global Game Jam 2024 - Make Me Laugh

You've Been Gnomed

Unity

Game made in 48h for the Global Game Jam with the theme Make Me Laugh. Your unic purpose is throwing gnomes to everything. Wining 2 prizes in Las Naves, Valencia.

Team

  • Aleksander Forca (Original idea)
  • Miguel Angel (Gnome Artist)
  • Marc Morlas (Programmer)
  • Carlos Cordero (Programmer)
  • Guillem Llorach (Programmer)

Mechanics

  • Grab & throwing Gnome.
  • Interact with objects throwing gnomes.

Download on Itchio


More info

The game goal is to create interactions with the gnomes and the environment until the most wanted gnome appears, and you have to find him. The game was made phisicaly made in Las Naves, Valencia, except for the artist, who was working remotely making an incredible number of accessories for gnomes.
