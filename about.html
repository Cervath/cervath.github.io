My name is Guillem Llorach Basset and I am from Andorra, a small country surrounded by mountains located between Spain and France. I have always loved video games and their unique way of expression, which is why I feel very fulfilled to be able to create, program, and design them.

I love making video games, so sometimes I create prototypes or release games on my own, but coming together with more people to work on a common project is a very stimulating and enriching experience, whether it's in game jams or more serious projects.

I would love to work with a team that is also passionate about video games and help create memorable experiences.

What am I working on right now?

At the moment, I am in Coventry finishing my degree in the Games Technology module, preparing for the new projects that are coming.

On the other hand, I’ve been experimenting with Godot, and I’m working on a very small game, focusing on the UX and the game feel.
